Earn

Souldrainer is different from other P2E and pre-2025 projects in its economy. Our economy is de-inflationary due to the protocols and mechanisms implemented, including the Oracle, which we described in detail here (Кликабельная ссылка).

Players primarily earn rewards by completing quests from NPCs, exploring new locations and participating in certain PvE raids. Completing these quests gives them "Gold", which can be converted into $SOUL. It is also worth stopping here to mention one of the reasons why the token will grow, which is the desire of players to earn more and faster by buying in-game items such as weapons and models that improve stats, kill bosses better, and complete missions faster, allowing them to earn more "Gold" tokens.

- What is Soul Drainer?

For fantasy fans — an immersive world with an engaging combat system, a wide variety of classes and mechanics, along with a captivating storyline.

For investors — a unique economic model, massive growth potential, and the transparency of our project.

For collectors — a multitude of characters, weapons, armor, and collectible items waiting for you to discover or acquire them.

How to earn?

The primary problem with conventional P2E games is inflation and falling token prices, which Soul Drainer solves using a deflationary economy driven by innovative protocols. Players receive in-game currency for activities, combat, and exploration that can be exchanged for $SOUL—a tradable token on DEXs and CEXs. The price oracle of the game guarantees stability by dynamically adjusting rewards and item prices according to the value of $SOUL, thus ensuring balance and fairness in the economy. Furthermore, the gas fees are kept below the potential earnings, hence players can continue to be profitable. With a limited supply of $SOUL and no mint function, the system has a complex relationship between player action and token value, thus ensuring a sustainable and accessible experience for both new and old players.
